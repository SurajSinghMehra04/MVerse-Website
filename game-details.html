About this game

Overview

Neon Drift Royale drops you into a sprawling open city under permanent neon dusk, where every street is a racetrack and every rival is a real player. Grab a vehicle, pick a lane, and find out who actually owns these streets.

Story

Years after the Grid Wars, the city's underground racing leagues rebuilt themselves around a single rule: speed is currency. You arrive with nothing but a borrowed car and a name to make.

Gameplay

Drift through checkpoints, slipstream past rivals, and bank reputation points that unlock new districts, vehicles, and customization options. Matches scale from quick 4-player sprints to full 16-player royale circuits.

Objectives

Finish top 3 to bank progress. Win outright to unlock that race's signature paint job. Climb the seasonal leaderboard to earn limited-time vehicle skins.

Tips

Hold drift through wide corners to build boost meter faster than braking. Use the minimap's heat trail to anticipate rival shortcuts before they take them.
